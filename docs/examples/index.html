Lyra Examples

Build traceable evidence graphs through AI conversation.

Reports and dashboards for sharing.

Features

  • Create Evidence Graph — Build traceable evidence with claim→fragment→page→URL links
  • Make Reports — Export findings with citations and confidence scores
  • Generate Dashboards — Interactive visualization comparing multiple research sessions
The Evidence Graph is the primary output — reports and dashboards are artifacts for sharing and audit.

Workflow

1

Build Evidence Graph

Ask a research question with /lyra-search. AI searches, extracts claims, and builds a traceable evidence graph.

2

Generate Report (optional)

Export to a structured report with /lyra-report. Citations and confidence scores included.

3

Generate Dashboard (optional)

Compare multiple sessions with make report-dashboard. Interactive HTML visualization.

Philosophy

Lyra's primary output is the Evidence Graph, not reports or visualizations.

The "main path" of Lyra is:

  • Grow an Evidence Graph — task → targets → pages/fragments/claims
  • Query the graph — views, search, contradictions, provenance — iteratively until the hypothesis is answered

Reports and dashboards serve shareability (portable artifacts), auditability (explicit provenance), and comparison (cross-session analysis).

The evidence you collect is yours — stored locally, queryable with standard tools, exportable in any format. Unlike cloud "deep research" that vanishes after a session, your Evidence Graph is a permanent, auditable asset.

⚠️ Disclaimer: These samples were created for demonstration purposes only. The reports and dashboard should not be treated as reliable medical information.
